# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this public feedback repository a welcoming, respectful, and constructive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful and considerate in your language
- Focus on the product issue or suggestion, not the person raising it
- Assume good intent and stay solution-oriented
- Use clear, helpful details when reporting problems
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive personal information

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable in this repository include:

- Harassment, threats, or abuse
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, insults, or mockery
- Repeatedly derailing or spamming issues
- Posting private, confidential, or sensitive information
- Intentionally disrupting discussion or moderation

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove or lock content, edit labels, or close issues that violate this code of conduct or do not fit the scope of the repository.

## Reporting Concerns

If you believe someone has violated this code of conduct, or if you need help with moderation, please contact the Sanehood team through the private support or moderation channel used by the project.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all participation in this repository, including issues, comments, and other public interactions connected to the project.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is based on the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
